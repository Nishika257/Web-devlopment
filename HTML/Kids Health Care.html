💪 Why Your Body Needs Exercise

Your body is like a supercar — the more you use it, the stronger it gets! Kids who move for 60 minutes everyday are happier ,sleep better,and do better at school.

Dancing, swiming, football, yoga — anithing that gets your heart beating faster counts. keep moving and have fun!

☀ Quick Health tips

  • 🍎 Eat 5 Fruits and veggies everyday
  • 💧 Drink 8 glasses of water daily
  • 😴 Sleep 9 to 11 hours every night
  • 🏃 move your body for 60 minutes
